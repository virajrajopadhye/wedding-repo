Our Story

  • First We Meet

    It feels like we’ve known each other since forever. I cannot pin the exact date when we first met as we practically grew up together. We were classmates since kindergarten! In the eighth grade I decided he was going to be my boyfriend and by tenth grade we knew we had found something really special. What started as a high-school crush, turned into a decade long romance. Be it our board exams, sneaky dates, moving to a new city, exploring bike rides, planning epic birthdays and talking for hours at a time, we’ve seen and done it all..

  • Journey

    It wasn’t always a bed of roses, sometimes it was a downright battlefield. But every time we drifted apart, we have found a way back to each other somehow, and I think that’s what love is all about.

  • How it happened..

    After going through this entire roller coaster ride, it all fell in place on January 6, 2021 when we met after two years, as he was in USA. We talked and talked and it was like no time had passed. So, we decided to talk some more for the next 6 months before we took any decisions, but by late February we were locked and loaded. Our parents and loved ones were over the moon!
